TowerLift is a one-thumb physics stacking game for iPhone and iPad. A crane swings each new floor over your tower — tap to drop it. Land it clean and climb higher; land it sloppy and the tower leans, wobbles… and comes crashing down.

Five difficulty levels, five sky worlds, daily challenges and Game Center leaderboards. Free to play, no pay-to-win — one optional purchase removes all forced ads forever.
