Frequently asked

  • I bought Remove Ads on a new device — how do I get it back? Open Settings inside the game and tap "Restore Purchases". Purchases are tied to your Apple Account.
  • Out of hearts? Hearts refill automatically over time. You can also watch an optional ad for a free heart, or buy an instant refill.
  • My score didn't appear on the leaderboard. Make sure you're signed in to Game Center in iOS Settings.
  • The game feels laggy. Close background apps and make sure iOS is up to date. TowerLift supports iPhone and iPad.

Report a bug

Email us with your device model, iOS version, and a short description (screenshots or a screen recording help a lot).
